# Alibaba Supplier Finder

Find reliable suppliers on 1688/Alibaba for your products.

## Description

This skill helps you source products from Chinese suppliers by:
- Identifying verified manufacturers
- Comparing prices and MOQs
- Evaluating supplier reliability
- Negotiation strategies

## Usage

Activate when user wants to:
- Find product suppliers
- Compare manufacturer prices
- Verify supplier credibility
- Calculate landed costs

## Workflow

1. **Product Specification** - Define exact product requirements
2. **Supplier Search** - Find matching suppliers on 1688/Alibaba
3. **Verification** - Check supplier credentials and history
4. **Price Comparison** - Compare quotes and MOQs
5. **Cost Calculation** - Calculate total landed cost

## Evaluation Criteria

- **Gold Supplier** status
- Years in business (3+ preferred)
- Transaction history
- Response rate and time
- Trade Assurance coverage

## Output Format

```markdown
## Supplier Report: [Product Name]

### Top 3 Suppliers

#### 1. [Supplier Name]
- Price: ¥XX/unit (MOQ: XXX)
- Location: [City, Province]
- Years: X years
- Rating: X.X/5
- Trade Assurance: Yes/No

### Cost Breakdown
- Unit Cost: ¥XX
- Shipping: ¥XX/unit
- Customs: ~X%
- **Landed Cost: $XX/unit**
```
